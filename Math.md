# Math

## Formeln

[[Gradmass]] $a$ zu [[Bogenmass]] $b = \frac{\pi\cdot\alpha}{180^\circ}$

[[Sinus]]: $\sin(\alpha) = \frac{GK}{H} = \frac{a}{c}$

[[Cosinus]]: $\cos(\alpha) = \frac{AK}{H} = \frac{b}{c}$

[[Tangens]]: $\tan(\alpha) = \frac{GK}{AK} = \frac{a}{b}=\frac{\sin(\alpha)}{\cos(\alpha)}$
